What SCIVIS is:

SCIVIS (Space Camp for Interested Visually Impaired Students) is a version of Space Camp, Space Camp Robotics, and Aviation Challenge for visually impaired students that takes place at the U.S. Space & Rocket Center (USSRC)

Scholarships:

Delta Gamma

Delta Gamma offers tuiton scholarships for students in the U.S. and Canada.


Northrop Grumman

Northrop Grumman offers a full scholarship for all students.


St. Louis Lighthouse for the Blind

The St. Louis Lighthouse for the Blind offers full scholarship for students with unique cultural lives (typically international students and students with unique heritage).


Local Organizations

Many civic organizations are open to funding the Space Camp Experience for blind and visually impaired children. Promising your funding organization a program at their weekly meeting when you return is advised to show appreciation.



*Full scholarships only cover tuition and base airfare not including things like airport food

Room and Board:

Chaperones, parents, and guests can stay at nearby hotels at their own expense. Campers must stay on site unless medical exemptions are required. The closest hotel is the Huntsville Marriott at the Space & Rocket Center located at 5 Tranquility Base. Group rates may be available on

our facebook page.
