What SCIVIS is:

SCIVIS (Space Camp for Interested Visually Impaired Students) is an adapted version of Space Camp, Space Camp Robotics, and Aviation Challenge's weeklong programs for visually impaired students.

Scholarships:

Delta Gamma

Delta Gamma offers tuiton scholarships for students in the U.S. and Canada.


Northrop Grumman

Northrop Grumman offers a full scholarship for all students.


St. Louis Lighthouse for the Blind

The St. Louis Lighthouse for the Blind offers full scholarship for students with unique cultural lives (typically international students and students with unique heritage).



*Full scholarships only cover tuition and base airfare not including things like airport food

Room and Board::

Chaperones, parents, and guests can stay at nearby hotels at their own expense. Campers must stay on site unless medical exemptions are required. The closest hotel is the Huntsville Marriott at the Space & Rocket Center located at 5 Tranquility Base. Group rates may be avaiable on

our facebook page.
