General Info

SCIVIS differs from the other camps as it accommodates far fewer students therefore students are housed in habitats 1 and 3 with habitat 2 being used for camp staff and services. Students only have about 45 minutes to get ready for the day and 45 to wind down in their rooms as the majority of the day is spent doing things around the campus with short breaks to rest or get a drink. Students are expected to participate in all activites that they are medically capable of as not everyone is able to attend SCIVIS.
